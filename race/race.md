# Human Races on Roshar

A guide to the ethnicities of Roshar for accurate fan casting.

## Key Races

### Alethi (Alethkar)
- **Characters:** Kaladin, Dalinar, Adolin, Jasnah, Navani
- **Physical Traits:** Tall stature, tan skin, black hair, epicanthic folds
- **Eyes:** Both lighteyes and darkeyes present
- **Real-World Inspiration:** Asian-Hawaiian / East Indian

### Vedens (Jah Keved)
- **Characters:** Shallan (has Horneater ancestry)
- **Physical Traits:** Fair skin, red hair possible (from Horneater ancestry)
- **Eyes:** Lighteyes and darkeyes
- **Real-World Inspiration:** Asian-Caucasian blend, leaning more Asian

### Unkalaki / Horneaters (Horneater Peaks)
- **Characters:** Rock, his family
- **Physical Traits:** Light tan/fair skin, red hair, large build, strong back molars
- **Note:** Parshendi hybrid ancestry
- **Real-World Inspiration:** Polynesian-Russian blend

### Herdazians (Herdaz)
- **Characters:** Lopen
- **Physical Traits:** Deep tan skin (darker than Alethi), dark crystalline fingernails
- **Note:** Parshendi hybrid ancestry
- **Real-World Inspiration:** Hispanic

### Thaylens (Thaylenah)
- **Characters:** Rysn, Vstim
- **Distinguishing Feature:** Long white eyebrows
- **Real-World Inspiration:** Unknown/Unique

### Natan (Former Natanatan)
- **Physical Traits:** Pale bluish skin, wide nose, wool-like white hair
- **Note:** Aimian hybrid ancestry

### Makabaki (Former Makabakam)
- **Characters:** Lift, Gawx, the Azish
- **Physical Traits:** Dark brown/black skin, black hair
- **Notable Subgroups:** Azish, Emuli, Tukari
- **Real-World Inspiration:** Sub-Saharan African

### Selay (Former Sela Tales)
- **Physical Traits:** Skin ripples
- **Subgroups:**
  - Purelakers: Leathery skin, dark hair, long limbs
  - Babath: Women display vein patterns beneath skin (Aimian heritage)

### Iriali (Iri/Rira)
- **Physical Traits:** Yellow eyes, golden skin (often painted), golden metallic-sheen hair
- **Note:** Completely unique - the golden hair is literally metallic

### Shin (Shinovar)
- **Characters:** Szeth
- **Physical Traits:** Short stature, pale skin, large round eyes, male baldness pattern
- **Key Note:** Lack epicanthic folds - the ONLY Rosharan ethnicity without them
- **Perception:** Often seen as "childlike" by other Rosharans due to round eyes

### Reshi (Reshi Isles)
- **Physical Traits:** Tan skin, straight black hair, rounded features
- **Real-World Inspiration:** Hispanic culture representation

## Important Context

> "Normal" eyes on Roshar have epicanthic folds. The Shin are the exception with round eyes - the opposite of Earth's demographics.

This means most characters (Kaladin, Dalinar, Adolin, etc.) should be cast with actors of Asian, Pacific Islander, or similar heritage for accuracy.
